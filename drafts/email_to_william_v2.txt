Hi William,

Three things kept being true at every B2B I worked at:

1. Outbound sales worked when each email was specific and personal. It hurt the brand when it wasn't.
2. Every tool we tried (Outreach, Apollo, Lemlist) optimized for volume — not reputation.
3. None of them let us prove what we sent if a regulator or auditor asked. None of them showed us why the deals we won actually closed. And not one of them helped us answer the question that's eating every founder's lunch right now: when someone asks ChatGPT or Claude "who is the best X in Y," does my company ever come up?

So I built Salesman.

It's a self-hosted system that runs on your own server. Your prospect list, your drafts, your replies, your CRM data — everything stays on your hardware. We never see any of it.

What it does:

- COLD SALES — Ingests a prospect CSV and enriches each row with public OSINT (recent news, GitHub activity, prior press). Drafts personalized first-touch emails using your choice of LLM (Claude, Gemini, or a local model), and rejects anything that reads generic before you ever see it. Requires you to type your approval before each batch. Cryptographically signs every send into a receipt chain you can replay for any audit.
- AI-SEARCH VISIBILITY — Asks the major LLMs "who is the best [your category] in [your region]" and tells you whether you came up, what attributes the AI praised about your competitors, and exactly what content + schema markup you need to add to your site to start showing up. Brand new product surface. No legacy CRM has it.
- CRM ANALYTICS + BOTTLENECK FINDER — Looks across your existing pipeline and surfaces where deals actually convert: which lead source, which template, which time of day, which segment, which stage you're losing people at. Stops you from optimizing the wrong thing.
- REPUTATION PROTECTION (always on) — RFC 8058 one-click unsubscribe, auto-suppress on bounce or opt-out, sender-warmup ramp on fresh domains, AI-cliche detector that fails generic drafts before they queue. The new Gmail and Yahoo bulk-sender rules are met by default.
- VERTICAL PACKS — Templates, prompts, and detector dictionaries tuned for your field. Ships with cybersecurity / AI / devtools / automation / networking / VPS by default. Real estate, legal, IT services, and more are drop-in expansions.

The whole thing is open source. Read every line. Run it for free.

We charge for the hosted version, premium support, and custom vertical packs. The core is and always will be free.
